Basketball Progression (2020)

Video Production/Video Editing
1920x1080
4:04

This is an experimental video that tells a story with footage I recorded and footage found on the internet. The clips were sequenced and edited in the Adobe program Premier Pro. The story shows the progression of a new player trying to get better at basketball. I recently started playing basketball again (one of my passions until recently), after a bit of a hiatus. The filming of this video helped rekindle my passion for the game and it also made me want to help others who might be interested starting out. The video consists of drills to help new players develop the basic skills to become better basketball players.
